About

Hello, and welcome to Melbourne // Naarm Gigs!

Have you ever found yourself looking for something to do on a weekend evening, but weren't quite sure where to start? Have you ever missed a band you really wanted to see because you didn't know they were in town? Or, just as frustratingly, tickets were sold out by the time you realised they'd be playing?

You're not alone - I've been there too. In fact, I got so darn diddly sick of it that I built this website to give live music FOMO the old left-right-goodnight.

While other live music hubs exist, I also wanted to make it easier for gig-goers to discover new music they might not have considered otherwise - Spotify links are displayed where an active artist is detected. As a result, your experience of this app will be best if you are logged into Spotify on your current device

Disclaimer: The artist matching an event may not always be correct - AI is used to determine the most likely artist given an event title. If your event is listed here and the corresponding artist/s Spotify is incorrect and you'd like to see it change, please log using the form in the contact page.

Otherwise I hope you get value from using this, and please reach out if you have any feedback or would just like to get in touch ☺️

Take care,

Cal
