FAQ


What is Melbourne // Naarm Gigs?

A website to help you find upcoming gigs happening around in and Melbourne (Naarm)!


Where does event info come from?

Events are gathered fom public ticketing platforms and venue websites. A sprinkle of AI helps match artists to their Spotify pages, helping you find new music!


Is everything 100% accurate?

We do our best to keep things up to date, but we recommend checking the official ticketing or venue link before heading out.

Moreover, AI is used to match an event title to a Spotify-active artist. Occasionally this is incorrect. If you notice an error you'd like to see change, please reach out via the contact form.


Can I suggest an event?

Currently this website picks up picking events automatically. However, if you spot something cool that's not listed, feel free to reach out!


Do you collect my data?

Heck no. This site doesn't track you. No collection of personal info or use of cookies. Browse gigs with peace of mind.


Can I scrape or reuse the listings?

The site is built for personal use. Please don't bulk scrape or republish the listings without asking first. Sharing with mates? Heckin' go for it!

If you would like to use these listings commercially or for a project, we may be able to provide you an API. Reach out!
